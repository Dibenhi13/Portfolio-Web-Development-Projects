Characters from my House

Draco Malfoy

A cunning and ambitious wizard from Slytherin House, Draco is known for his sharp wit, pride in his pure-blood heritage, and rivalry with Harry Potter.

Severus Snape

A brave and complex Slytherin who mastered Potions and Defense Against the Dark Arts. His loyalty and sacrifice reveal true courage beneath his stern exterior.

Tom Riddle (Voldemort)

Once a brilliant student, Tom Riddle became Lord Voldemort — the darkest wizard of all time, whose ambition and thirst for power knew no bounds.

Slytherin House

• Ambition • Cunning • Determination • Resourcefulness •

Slytherin was founded by Salazar Slytherin, a cunning and ambitious wizard who valued cleverness, ambition, determination, and blood purity.

Historically, the house has been home to powerful and often controversial wizards, including figures such as Severus Snape and Tom Riddle (Voldemort).
